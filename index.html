MY PROJECTS

I previously worked as a marketing executive for FPT and in business development with Shinhan Bank before finding my passion in yoga, travel and digital marketing. I left office life in 2014 and began travelling the world, becoming a yoga yeacher, travel blogger and digital nomad. Currently I'm working on 3 big projects.

Yogi Travel

www.yogitravelvn.com

Yogi Travel is my blog where I share my travel and yoga journey. After 3 years, the blog now has over 200 articles about yoga and travel generating 10k views a month with over 50 articles reaching the first page of Google search. Besides the blog, I also run Yogi Travel's Instagram page and YouTube channel. Yogi Travel is how I share my passion for travel and yoga, inspiring others to live the life they dream of.

Zoom Yoga Saigon

www.zoomyoga.com

Zoom Yoga Saigon is a private yoga studio, filled with plants and soothing energy. I named it Zoom because I want people to "zoom in" to see themself, to take care of their mind and body, and to "zoom out" to think outside the box. In 4 years, Zoom Yoga has helped over 200 women transform to become the best version of themself.
