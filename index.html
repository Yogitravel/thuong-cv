I gave up my office life since May 2014 and start travelling the world, become a Yoga Teacher, a Travel Blogger and a Digital Nomad. Here are 3 big projects that I am working on.

Yogi Travel

www.yogitravelvn.com

Yogi Travel is a blog page where I share my travel and yoga journey. After 3 years, the blog now have 200 articles about yoga and travel with about 10k views a month and over 50 articles are on the first page of Google search. Beside the website blog page, I also run the Instagram page which now have 12k followers and YouTube page which have 10k subcribres this year. Yogi Travel is a chanel for me to share my passion about travel and yoga, also to inspride people to have the life that they want.

Zoom Yoga Saigon

www.zoomyoga.com

Zoom Yoga Saigon is a small Yoga Studio, full of plants and great viber. I named it Zoom because I want people to "zoom in" to see themself, to takecare their mind and their body, and "zoom out" to think out of the box, to discover the universal. After 4 years, Zoom Yoga has been working with over 200 womens, transfer them to become a better version of themself.
